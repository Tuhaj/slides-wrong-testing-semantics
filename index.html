Why could be naming important?

  1. Helps understand the code
  2. Explains, when uses known concepts from other fields
  3. Adds culture

The problem with application testing sematics

  • There are basically two results: PASS and FAIL
  • Is PASS a positive or negative test result?
  • What does it mean that a testing suite gives you a false positive result?

Examples from different fields

  • HIV positive test
  • Lyme disease (Lyme borreliosis)
  • The man was not planning any manslaughter and was innocent, the algorithm gave us a false positive

Source:

Amazon

False positive result is when:

  • app works but tests are failing?
  • app is broken but tests are passing?

Source: Wikipedia: Sensitivity_and_specificity

If we fixed our naming and thinking we could say:

  • tests are too sensitive, give us false positives
  • tests are not enough sensitive they give us false negatives
  • tests are too specific, they give us false negatives
  • tests are not enough specific, they give us false positives
